The best movies according to Kunal

My top 3 movies of all time

3 Idiots

It is the best movie i have ever watched in my life. Despite being a bit old, this movie has continued to entertain everyone even to this date.This movie teaches us to run behind excellence, and not success.

Chhichhore

Really funny, and again, conveys a great message to everyone: your result does not decide whether you are a winner or a loser, but your hardwork does.

12th Fail

Really motivating and inspiring movie. The story revolves around a boy, who fails in 12th standard, but because of his hardwork and dedication, he ends up being an IPS officer.
